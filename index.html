How do I rent a GPU on BNB Edge Node? Ôºã
Connect MetaMask or WalletConnect to opBNB (Chain ID 204). Browse GPU providers on the marketplace ‚Äî filter by GPU model (H100, A100, RTX 4090), price and reputation. What is a claw bot marketplace and how does it work? Ôºã
A claw bot marketplace is a decentralised platform to buy, sell, deploy and monetise automated bots ‚Äî including DeFi arbitrage bots, NFT snipers, data scrapers and Web3 automation agents. On BNB Edge Node, developers list their claw bots with per-hour or per-execution pricing. Buyers deploy with a single click and pay via streaming BNB smart contract payments. No escrow delays ‚Äî payments stream per second directly to bot developers.
What is DePIN and why does it matter for AI compute? Ôºã
DePIN (Decentralised Physical Infrastructure Network) coordinates real hardware ‚Äî servers, GPUs, network gear ‚Äî owned by independent providers, incentivised on-chain. For AI compute, DePIN means lower prices (no AWS margin), censorship resistance (no ToS bans), and transparent SLAs enforced by smart contracts rather than cloud provider policies. BNB Edge Node uses DePIN to deliver GPU compute at up to 60% less than AWS.
How fast are payments on opBNB and what is the gas cost? Ôºã
opBNB achieves 0.45-second transaction finality with gas costs of approximately $0.001 per transaction. This makes per-second micropayment streaming economically viable ‚Äî you can stream 1 BNB payment per second and pay less than $0.001 in gas per stream tick. This is why BNB Edge Node chose opBNB over Ethereum mainnet or other L1s for its payment infrastructure.
